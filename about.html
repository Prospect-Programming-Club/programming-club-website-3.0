About Us

This is what I do.

At Prospect Programming Club, all our members collaborate to create various projects (individually or in groups) to show our knowledge of different programming languages that we have learned over the time here at this club. Our projects can range from games, websites, and other user interactive tools.

A major event that our members attend are Hackathons, where we can present our amazing programming knowledge to other people as well as learn more from other groups and experienced helped there. Hackathons could be online or in-person, but they are awlays a great bonding and learning time for everybody! No prior experience of programmnig is needed to attend Hackathons or join our club because at the end of the day, we are all here to learn.

Our club meetings consist of lessons and/or working time for projects. It is a great way to share knowledge with other people and make new friends. Everybody is welcome to the Prospect Programming club!
