About Us

At Prospect Programming Club, all our members collaborate to create various projects (individually or in groups) to show our knowledge of different programming languages that we have learned over the time here at this club. Our projects can range from games, websites, and other user interactive tools.

A major event that our members attend are hackathons, where we can present our amazing programming knowledge to other people as well as learn more from other groups and experienced helped there. Hackathons could be online or in-person, but they are always a great bonding and learning time for everybody! No prior experience of programmnig is needed to attend hackathons or join our club because at the end of the day, we are all here to learn.

Our club meetings consist of lessons and/or working time for projects. It is a great way to share knowledge with other people and make new friends. Everybody is welcome to the Prospect Programming Club!

What We Do

In the Programming Club, we learn many different computer languages and concepts - from the actual syntax code to the main ideas behind it, we explore the in-depth knowledge of mutliple well-known computer languages. We also participate in fun Hackathons to gain more experience and compete. Based on what we have learned, we devise and complete in projects with code. Programming Club here at Prospect is extremely flexible and it an amazing time! Whatever your past expereince is with programming, this club will help teach the basics / complex topics of computer languages.

Who We Are
